Features

CAMPUS RECRUITMENT TRAINING

It is exclusively designed for the students preparing for recruitment and is geared towards ensuring that the students are well equipped to get through the process of various IT and Non-IT companies by gathering the inputs from Job-seekers and Placement providers.

CONTENT DEVELOPMENT

Analyze and research exam patterns, types of questions and difficulty level to remain updated with latest trends in questions asked in competitive exams. Plan, develop and execute launch of new test series, in coordination with our team members.

PERSONALITY GROOMING

Considering the vitality of personality in life, some necessary and result oriented tips are given. Developments of interaction skills, short and to the point way of communication, positivity in outlook, etc. are the key focus areas.

PLACEMENT WORKSHOP

To prepare students to face job interviews & group discussions. Students are taught skills like resume writing, presentation skills and time management etc. Make students completely industry-ready by the time they complete their respective course.

EXAMINATION CONSULTING

Seminars on importance of balanced preparation for competitive exams, other than Campus recruitment tests, conducted by various Government organizations. Making the students aware of all the patterns and nature of exams, and most importantly the strategy and the right approach to nail these exams.
